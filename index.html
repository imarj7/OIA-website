About DTU

Delhi Technological University (formerly Delhi College of Engineering) has an illustrious history spanning over 80 years. This prestigious institution is well known throughout the world for its world class education and student centric research & innovations. In this university the culture of research and innovations is inculcated right from the UG level. We have briliant faculty and highly distinguished alumni who have made a mark worldwide. It is a testament to the ability of the university in educating future leaders.

Mode of Admission

Out of the 15% seat on supernumerary basis, three modes of admission of Foreign Nationals/Indian Nationals studying abroad/Overseas Citizen of India are prescribed.

Learn More

ICCR

Under this category the application of the candidates shall be routed directly through Indian Council of Cultural Relation (ICCR).(link: http://a2ascholarships. iccr.gov.in/)

DTU Portal

Under this category candidates shall apply on-line in the prescribed format before the last date directly on the web portal of the office of International Affairs (link: https://dtu.ac.in/).

DASA

Under this category the student directly apply Complete through DASA portal. Complete information regarding the procedure of admission, eligibility and payment of fee will be available on DASA website (link: http:/www/dasanit.org)

Frequently Asked Questions

How to get admission as an international Student at DTU?

You can take admission at DTU via 3modes. Download Brochure for more details.

Is JEE mains mandatory for B.Tech at DTU?

JEE mains is mandatory for admission through DASA,and DTU portal. As of now, JEE mains isn't mandatory for ICCR applicants, however, preference is given to those with JEE mains score card.

Is there any international hostel at DTU?

Yes, We have an international hostel at DTU, which can fulfill all your needs.
